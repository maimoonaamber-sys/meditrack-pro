# **App Name**: MediTrack Pro

## Core Features:

- Medicine Input and Info: Allows users to input medicines they are taking and displays purpose, benefits, and side effects using the Gemini tool and extracting article data from Google Search
- Sickness History Tracking: Tracks the user's history of illnesses, including the duration of each illness.
- Symptom Recording and Comparison: Allows users to record symptoms and compares these against a database to identify potential serious conditions.
- Medical History Review: Provides a comprehensive review of the userâ€™s medical history, including past illnesses, medications, and treatment outcomes.
- Test Report Integration: Enables users to upload lab reports and interprets key results to assess health risks.
- Alerts and Recommendations: Notifies users of potential health risks based on symptoms, medication, or test results, suggesting medical consultation if needed.
- User-Friendly Health Dashboard: Displays personalized health insights, trends over time, and risk scores for potential conditions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to evoke trust and stability.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F2F5) to ensure a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Teal (#009688) to highlight important alerts and recommendations.
- Body font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font that will offer a neutral and objective feel. Headlines: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, for a techy and scientific feel
- Use clear and intuitive icons to represent different health metrics and actions.
- A clean and structured layout that emphasizes key health metrics and provides easy navigation.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
